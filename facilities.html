Facilities

Miserior Building

One of the original structures in PUP Quezon City, now an integral part of the campus where Learning Resource Center, and academic and administrative offices for students and faculty members can be found.

Director's Office

The Office of the Director provides and oversees academic programs and administrative leadership, engages with stakeholders, ensures compliance, represents the campus, and maintains a positive environment for the campus community.

Admission Office

The admission office of PUP strives to facilitate the enrollment process and ensure equal access to quality education for all prospective students.

Office of Academic Program

The Office of the Academic Program aims to oversee and enhance the academic quality of the program, ensuring that it meets the standards of excellence and prepares students for successful careers in their chosen field.

Collecting and Disbursement

The Collecting and Disbursementâ€™s Office is responsible for processing financial transactions, collecting fees and payments, and maintaining accurate financial records to ensure the smooth and efficient operation of the institution.

Office of Student Affairs and Services

The Office of Student Affairs and Services is dedicated to providing comprehensive support services and programs that enhance the holistic development of students, foster their personal and academic growth, and promote a safe, inclusive, and thriving campus community.

Quality Assurance Office

The Quality Assurance Office is committed to ensuring and enhancing the academic excellence and integrity of the institution through the development, implementation, and monitoring of quality assurance policies, procedures, and standards.

Learning Resource Center

The Learning Resource Center (LRC) serves as a hub of academic resources and services, providing students with access to various learning materials, technologies, and facilities to support their learning and research needs, and to promote lifelong learning.

Guidance and Counseling Office

The Guidance and Counseling Office provides a range of counseling services and resources to support the personal, social, and emotional well-being of students, and to assist them in achieving their academic and personal goals.

Student Records Section

The Student Record Section is responsible for the accurate and secure management of student academic records, including enrollment, grades, transcripts, and graduation requirements, and for providing efficient and timely services to students, faculty, and staff regarding academic records.

Faculty Room

The Faculty Room is a place for faculty members to collaborate, work, and communicate. It is used for meetings, working areas, resource storage, and professional development.

Audio Visual Room

The Audio-Visual Room is a specialized facility equipped with advanced multimedia technologies and resources to enhance the teaching and learning experience, facilitate presentations and lectures, and promote interactive and engaging learning activities.

Computer Laboratory Room

The Computer Lab Room is a dedicated facility equipped with state-of-the-art computing technologies and software, providing students with access to resources and tools for research, projects, and coursework, and promoting digital literacy and technological proficiency.

Academic Building

An academic building is a physical space designed and equipped to support various academic activities, including teaching, learning, research, and administration, and to provide a conducive environment for academic excellence and innovation.

Interfaith Chapel

An Interfaith Chapel is a sacred space designed to provide a welcoming environment for people of all faiths and beliefs to come together for prayer, reflection, and meditation, fostering spiritual growth, understanding, and respect for diversity.

University Security and Safety Office

Rothlehner Building

Records Management Office

The Records Management Office is responsible for the efficient and effective management of the institution's records and information, including their creation, organization, maintenance, retrieval, and disposition, ensuring their accuracy, authenticity, confidentiality, and accessibility, and complying with relevant laws and regulations.

Medical Clinic

The Medical Clinic is a facility equipped with medical professionals and resources to provide a range of health services and programs to promote and maintain the physical and mental well-being of students, faculty, and staff, and to support a healthy campus environment.

Department of Military Science and Tactics

The Department of Military Science and Tactics is a specialized academic unit that offers courses and programs in military science, leadership, and strategy, providing students with the knowledge, skills, and values necessary for a career in the military or other related fields.
